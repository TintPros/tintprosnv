History: TintPros would like to thank you for taking interest in our company and our products. We have been in the window film business since the early 70s. Over the many years we have always stayed on the cutting edge of the window film industry, testing new products and evaluating the performance and longevity of the different window film materials on the market.
In doing this, we can assure that our customers are purchasing a window film that will be trouble-free for the warranty period that is offered by the manufacturers.

 

Reputation: Our company has built a reputation on the high quality of our film installations. The time that we put into our finished product assures that the customer will be more than satisfied. All of our film patterns are cut from a computer to assure a precise fit for all of our window film installations.

 

Estimates: If you would like to schedule an appointment or learn more about our products give us a call. A friendly TintPros applicator will be happy to answer any questions you may have. Average time for automotive window tint is 1 1/2 hours.

Estimates for business and residential tinting can be given over the phone by giving us the height and width and number of windows to be done.

 

Auto films
High performance charcoal by global window films comes in light, medium dark and limo this film comes with a lifetime warranty and covers bubbling peeling or color change in the material. It has high heat rejection as well as a non-reflective look

Black ceramic is our ultra-premium automotive film. It also comes in light medium dark and limo this film does not contain any metal it is made up of nano ceramic particles as well as infrared blocker coatings. The technology behind this film enables it to reduce extreme high amounts of heat with no reflective appearance. This film is signaled friendly. It will not interfere with GPS cell phone radio signals or any other electronic devices in your car.

Residential commercial films.
