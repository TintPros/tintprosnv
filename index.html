History: TintPros would like to thank you for taking interest in our company and our products. We have been in the window film business since the early 70s. Over the many years we have always stayed on the cutting edge of the window film industry, testing new products and evaluating the performance and longevity of the different window film materials on the market. In doing this, we can assure that our customers are purchasing a window film that will be trouble-free for the warranty period that is offered by the manufacturers.

New windshield sun control film from LLumar
LLumar, a world leader in the manufacturing of window films has brought to the marketplace a truly unique film.  Designed to give protection from the harsh sun entering through the vehicles front windshield.  For most cars and trucks, the front windshield is by far the largest piece of glass on the vehicle and transmits high amounts of solar heat and ultraviolet rays.  With LLumar AIR80 the reduction of solar heat will be 43% and ultraviolet will  be 99% and still maintains excellent vision at night.
