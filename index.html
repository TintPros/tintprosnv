History

TintPros would like to thank you for taking interest in our company and our products. We have been in the window film business since the early 70s. Over the many years we have always stayed on the cutting edge of the window film industry, testing new products and evaluating the performance and longevity of the different window film materials on the market. In doing this, we can assure that our customers are purchasing a window film that will be trouble-free for the warranty period that is offered by the manufacturers.

Reputation

Our company has built a reputation on the high quality of our film installations. The time that we put into our finished product assures that the customer will be more than satisfied. All of our film patterns are cut from a computer to assure a precise fit for all of our window film installations.

Estimates

If you would like to schedule an appointment or learn more about our products give us a call. A friendly TintPros applicator will be happy to answer any questions you may have. Average time for automotive window tint is 1 1/2 hours.
