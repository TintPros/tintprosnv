New windshield sun control film from LLumar
LLumar, a world leader in the manufacturing of window films has brought to the marketplace a truly unique film.  Designed to give protection from the harsh sun entering through the vehicles front windshield.  For most cars and trucks, the front windshield is by far the largest piece of glass on the vehicle and transmits high amounts of solar heat and ultraviolet rays.  With LLumar AIR80 the reduction of solar heat will be 43% and ultraviolet will  be 99% and still maintains excellent vision at night. Geoshield's DARKMATTER Films use the latest in science and nanoceramic technology to create a new hybrid film that will far outperform conventional dyed or dyed metalized films. DARKMATTER Films are engineered combining a color stable polyester film with similar ceramic materials used by NASA on the space shuttle. This unique hybrid combination allows these durable films to have excellent infrared (IR) reflectivity properties, reflecting the spectrum of the sun's rays that carry the most heat. Providing outstanding solar heat rejection by using nanoceramic particles to selectively filter out infrared heat at the glass, keeping you and your car cooler under the sun, blocking out up to 86% of that heat from ever entering your car.
